What is Excercise app?

Excercise app is designed to replace your paper workout journal. Originally inspired from a MERN stack application. Using Angular to power its front-end and express to supply its backend!

Made with love

This webapp is a simple excercise tracker, designed with adding users, so you can use it for yourself or with friends
